### Strategy Summary

**Indicators**:
- EMA(5) vs EMA(13)
- RSI(14)

**Signal Logic**:
- UP: EMA5 > EMA13 AND RSI < 75
- DOWN: EMA5 < EMA13 AND RSI > 25
- SKIP: No clear signal

**Position Sizing**:
- 50-69% confidence: 3 orders, 1.0x multiplier
- 70-84% confidence: 4 orders, 1.5x multiplier
- 85%+ confidence: 5 orders, 2.0x multiplier
